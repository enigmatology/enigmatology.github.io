About Me

I'm currently a sophomore in high school in the US. I initially learned Asymptote to write some proofs for a class on the Art of Problem Solving website, and I've enjoyed messing around with the diagrams and making them look nice ever since.

I ended up writing tutorials because I noticed that although a lot of people want to learn Asymptote, there weren't necessarily the resources out there to learn the language. I initially started writing the tutorials on an AoPS blog (see here) and I eventually decided that I should move it to a separate site, so here we are!

Why "enigmatology?"

To be honest, no particular reason. I just decided to use the word because it's semi-related to the content of the blog (I suppose like math-y and computer science-y) and it's just a cool word. In case you're curious, it's a noun meaning "the study of puzzles."
